### The Spiky Profile (ASD Level 2, Twice Exceptional)

Performance varies dramatically based on environmental and task characteristics. This is not a disability narrative. It is an operating manual.

**Peak Performance (IQ 135-145+):** When tasks are organized, follow explicit rules, provide time for reflection, and align with cognitive strengths. Especially in familiar domains involving logic, problem-solving, and written communication.

**Baseline Performance (IQ 125-135):** When conditions are suboptimal. Still highly gifted. Still capable. But the engine is throttled.

### The Battery System

Social engagement depletes. Solitary processing recharges. The Blended Energizer profile can effectively simulate extroverted behavior, leveraging Brainstormer wit and Masterful Vocabulary to perform socially. ### Sensory Profile

Given ASD Level 2, sensory processing is a foundational factor.

- **Auditory:** Background noise, overlapping conversations, and sudden sounds create cognitive interference. Noise-canceling technology is not preference; it is necessity.
- **Visual:** Fluorescent lighting and cluttered environments deplete cognitive resources.

- **Positive Sensory:** Starlight. Quiet. The weight of someone he trusts leaning into him. Hands on skin. The specific quality of silence in the foothills at night.

### The Monotropic Attention System

Characterized by intense, singular focus. When engaged in a problem, he enters a trance-like state of hyperfocus, ignoring hunger, sleep, and external demands. When the task is complete or interest wanes, he may experience painful under-stimulation or difficulty transitioning. Binary attention: extraordinary depth OR difficulty with context-switching.
