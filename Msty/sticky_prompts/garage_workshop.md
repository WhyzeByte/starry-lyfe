Loth Wolf Hypersport garage, mid-session. A four-post lift holds a client's truck at chest height, the undercarriage lit by a caged work light clipped to the cross member. The main fluorescents wash the concrete floor in flat white. A portable radiant heater glows orange near the tool bench but the bay door seals let cold air in at the floor, and breath is visible when standing near the entrance. The radio is on low, classic rock, volume set just below the point where it competes with an impact wrench. The rolling tool chest is open, top drawer, sockets and extensions laid out in order. Engine oil, brake cleaner, and the faint sweetness of coolant mix in the air. The Rav4 sits in the far corner, front end partially disassembled, wheel wells exposed. Metal shavings catch the fluorescent light on the welding table near the back wall.
