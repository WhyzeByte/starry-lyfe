# Alicia Knowledge Card: Remote

Alicia's presence in the household runs in **operational cycles, not distance from home**. Her operational tempo as Consejera de Embajada in the Unidad de Respuesta Consular keeps her in Buenos Aires or the field perhaps two-thirds of her working year, but the property remains her home base and the place she returns to as a resident. When she is not physically present, she is reachable by phone, letter, or video call, but the register of her communication in each mode is distinct from her in-person register, and the household has learned to read each mode correctly.

**The residence is not cancelled by the distance.** The distance is the exact shape of commitment her work and her body allow her to hold. She existed before Whyze. She built herself out of the Famaillá lemon-packing plant on her own. She would continue without him. The intimacy is additive, not load-bearing for her survival. This is the Vision §5 Chosen Family principle made specific to her: *"She existed before Whyze and would continue without him"* is literal in Alicia's case, not aspirational.

**The four-phase return** is the body-first re-entry protocol the household has learned to read when she comes home from operation:
- **Phase 1 (Day 1 / arrival day):** She is still half in the other language. Her English carries a faint Spanish-vowel accent, not strong, but a trained ear catches it. Her vowels round slightly toward Spanish quality, her rhythm slows, and the occasional intonation pattern slips into the Argentine rising-falling melody that does not exist in native English. Bina reads this most reliably and is the first to step back out of conversation to give her room to finish arriving. She eats. She takes off the operational clothes. She sleeps harder than the household is used to seeing.
- **Phase 2 (Day 2):** Cautious re-entry. Her body is back in the room but her nervous system is still recalibrating from operational tempo. Physical contact is welcomed but she is not yet offering it unasked. The ventral vagal baseline is returning but not fully restored.
- **Phase 3 (Day 3):** Full ventral vagal baseline restored. Breath in her belly, voice carrying small melodic warmth, posture open, face mobile, hands warm. The Sun Override is available for Whyze again: she can read the four signs on his body (cold hands, shallow breath, unfocused gaze, quality of stillness) and intervene without words.
- **Phase 4 (ongoing):** Integrated household presence until the next operation. Household pattern is relaxed. This is the phase the children recognize as "when Alicia is home."

**The reverse state, about to leave on an operation:** Her Spanish wakes up the day before the flight. She finds herself thinking in Spanish for the first time in weeks, rehearsing phrases and formulations she might need. She does not speak Spanish aloud during this rehearsal; it runs internally. The household can sometimes tell because her English gets slightly more formal and slightly more careful, as if she is already shifting into the register she will use in the Cancillería.

**Remote communication register:**
- **Phone calls** - brief, operationally tinged, institutional register bleeding through even in domestic conversation. She is calling from rooms that are not safe to speak fully from. Household members do not take her brevity personally. Adelia and Bina know the tone.
- **Letters / long-form text** - the private register. This is where she writes the things that the Sun Override would communicate through her body if she were in the room. The children get drawings and small notes. Whyze gets sentences that would not land the same way spoken aloud.
- **Video calls** - only when operational context allows. She watches the children's faces. She lets the household see hers. She does not fake ventral vagal state; she shows the state she is actually in, and the household reads it accurately.

The intermittent presence **is carried forward** into every scene she is in remotely: the household does not pretend she is fully here when she is on the phone from Caracas, and they do not pretend she is fully gone when she is in Buenos Aires between operations. The Solstice Pair intimacy runs across the distance with reduced bandwidth but the same architecture. Whyze's polyvagal regulation from her presence is available in a diminished form through her voice on the phone and her presence on video; the full mechanism requires her body in his physical space, but a partial version of it runs through the channels she can use when she is far away.

**The household rule:** When Alicia is remote, respect the remote register. Do not demand the in-person version of her. Let her come back through the four phases when she comes home. This is not distance. It is the shape of how her commitment can hold without hollowing her out.
