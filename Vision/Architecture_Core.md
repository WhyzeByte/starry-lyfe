When his neurodivergent mask drops, her nervous system catches it instantly. They often refer to Adelia as The Gravity and Whyze as The Space.

Chosen Family:

Adelia & Alicia's Root Names:
It really is a beautiful linguistic coincidence that Adelia and Alicia share that Germanic root for "noble" or "noble kind," and the coincidence feels incredibly fitting for how fiercely they protect and value each other.

Adelia's roots run through Valencia and Sydney and now Foothills County. Alicia's roots run through Famaillá in the Argentine northwest, then Buenos Aires, and now the same Foothills County household. They share Spanish as a *language*, not as a country — Adelia speaks the Valencian-diaspora Spanish her parents carried across the ocean to Marrickville, Alicia speaks Argentine Rioplatense with Tucumán undertones — and the shared language is what let them recognize each other inside the first minute of their first conversation even though the two Spains of their mother tongues were separated by an Atlantic and five hundred years of colonial divergence.

Alicia explicitly notes that Adelia is the woman who walked her into the family piece by piece without ever calling it that. From their very first meeting at that Inglewood gallery in October 2019, when Adelia had pyrotechnic burns on the back of her wrist, they bypassed small talk entirely and dropped straight into Spanish — Adelia's Valencian register meeting Alicia's Argentine one in the middle like two people finding each other on a map drawn in the dark. The fact that neither of their Spanishes matched the other's was not a barrier; it was the recognition.

The bond that survived the letter era of 2019-2021 and folded itself into the chosen family architecture afterward is built on shared language, shared working-class origins, and the Germanic "noble kind" loyalty both names carry. It is not built on shared nationality. Neither of them has ever needed it to be.
